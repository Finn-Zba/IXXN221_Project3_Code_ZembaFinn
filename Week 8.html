Week 8

Week 8 was a heavy week.

Style guide

First off the development of a style guide, which alike the mobile low-fidelity frames, I was less aware of. As I had already developed the home page, the style guide was pretty straightforward to make as it had already been implemented.

Prototyping

FAs well as finishing the home page, I then developed the other 3 main pages (Tool, library, contact) into my HIFI prototype. The contact page and the tool page translated well from my lo-fi wireframes, however, the library page went through changes to better fit the style of the prototype. This change left the library page really rough in its final prototype format but understandable for its purpose.

The Tool

It is commonly stated that design is the middle ground between Art and engineering. Here for the website purpose, I found myself pulled towards the extremes of both sides. Here I had to make the tool functional for my personal (Jessie Blur) while also making it look good. Utilizing different pages and overlays I managed to simulate my idea with the prototype, however to a more functional than perfectly designed in line to finish assignment 2 In time. And with that Assignment 2 comes to a close.
