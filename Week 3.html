Week 3

Who is Jessie Blur?

Here we have the start of the creative process that'll last the next 12 weeks.

WhooHoo!

The logo:

Starting with a logo we have 2 Ds cascading to create a book. the two Ds are the starting letters in Dyslexic Drive. The book symbol is a double meaning to represent reading and literacy for the intended demographic of neurodivergent readers.

Color pallete:

This color palette is based on the knowledge I had from the previous experience that would work best for me. A nice pastel pink is my preference to read off, hence the decision to make it the primary color.

Jessie blur:

Here for this class, I thought I'd do something I'm both somewhat familiar with and invested in and create something innovative and unique. Here I wanted to create a website format that would be able to cater to a group that normally requires sacrifices and affordances in accordance with a generic stimulating design. Here I would have a heavy challenge but also plenty of room to learn and expand my creativity with the class. Hence the birth of Jessie Blur, a professional and successful guy with a good career with one common disability holding him back. Something very prevalent in people with dyslexia. Jessie Blur as a concept is meant to represent the competent but held-back dyslexics of society who need very little change in the online world to unlock their true potential.

(fun fact - the name is based on the band I was listening to as I was working on it at the time - Blur)
