Week 6 + break

The LOFI and the break
Week 6

Week 6 Week 6 was the introduction to assignment 2, which is exciting to me as I love utilizing Figma to build both low and high-fidelity wireframes/ digital prototypes. However with my excitement also came negligence and procrastination as within week 6, not a lot was done. Not until week 2 of the study break at least. However, as an introduction to assignment 6, I'm so excited to see how it goes.

The break

Week 1 of the break was also unproductive… OOPS. Week 2 of the break however provided actual results. Here I would start the development of my low-fidelity desktop wireframes. I started with a simple home/index page. This was meant to be more appealing then functional as well as give the basic info of the site. After that I expanded to the tool page. The development of the tool page was surprisingly the easiest as its something I've wanted to do since before my time at VUW. With this a text description of how to utilize the tool and its purpose. As well as the 2 primary pages, I did a bit of animation and functionality to better make it flow.
